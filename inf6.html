Distance

The outermost known planet in the system is the only one in the habitable zone, it is 20% larger than Earth and receives 86% of the energy from its star than the Sun provides to Earth. The planet is 0.072 AU from its star, which is approximately 1/3 of the distance from Earth to the Sun. This means that the planet orbits its star in a period of 37.6 Earth days.
